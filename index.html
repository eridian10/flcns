Why Falcons United

More than football.

不只是足球。

🗣️

English Immersion

100% English coaching in a warm, encouraging environment. Perfect for international school kids — and those who want to build confidence in English.

全英語環境，適合就讀國際學校或希望加強英語自信的孩子。

🏆

Character First

We teach respect, independence, and teamwork — not just technique. Every coach believes in building better people, not just better players.

我們培養尊重、獨立與團隊合作，不只是技術。

🤝

A community families stay in

From tournaments to beach cleanups — Falcons is a community that goes beyond Saturday training. Children and parents quickly get to know other families through training, tournaments, and events.

從比賽日到海灘淨灘，Falcons 是一個真正的家庭社群。

For Parents

Good to know before you join.

🤒

Sick Day Policy

Each student gets 1 sick day credit per semester. Notify via LINE at least 12 hours before class to apply it.

每學期 1 次病假折抵，需提前 12 小時透過 LINE 請假。

💰

Cancellation & Refunds

Cancel before the semester starts for a full refund. Within the first 2 weeks: 80% back. After that: no refund.

開學前取消全額退款；兩週內退 80%；之後恕不退費。

🌧️

Weather Cancellations

Classes cancelled due to weather will always be rescheduled or refunded. We'll notify you in advance via LINE.

天氣取消課程將另安排補課，或退還該堂費用。
